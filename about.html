The Journey

The Business of Design

My path into design started on the business side. For 3.5 years, I managed EMEA operations for top-tier strategy consulting clients at Dialectica — leading a 15-person team, owning multi-market accounts, and driving 30% year-over-year revenue growth.

That experience gave me something I carry into every design decision: I don’t just think about how users interact with a product—I think about why it exists, what outcomes matter, and where the real friction is.

When I transitioned into design, I brought that perspective with me. Today, I design AI-powered tools for the same workflows I once managed.

The result is design grounded in operational reality and measured by real impact.
